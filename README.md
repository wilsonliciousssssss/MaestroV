# Maestro V

**ΛΩ · Alpha Omega Collective**

**Free audio-reactive visuals for your music — right in your browser.**

Play your music out loud, let Maestro V listen, and watch it paint the sound: matrix rain, fireworks, kaleidoscopes, galaxies and resonance patterns, all moving to your bass, melodies and hi-hats. No install, no account, nothing to download.

### ▶ Open it now: **https://wilsonliciousssssss.github.io/MaestroV/**

## Try it in 30 seconds

1. Open the link on your phone, tablet or computer.
2. Tap **Start Mic** and allow the microphone.
3. Play your music, go fullscreen, enjoy.

Your audio never leaves your device — Maestro V only *listens* locally to drive the visuals. Nothing is recorded, uploaded or tracked. No ads.

**Want it as an app?** Open the link on your phone → **Add to Home Screen** (Android: ⋮ menu · iPhone: Share button). You'll get the ΛΩ icon, it launches fullscreen, and it keeps working offline.

## What you'll see — 15 scenes

Matrix Coding · Code Glitch Terminal · Data Constellation · DNA Oscilloscope · Orbit Geometry · Mobius Galaxy · Obsidian Graph · Pixel Missile Barrage · Laser Perspective · Chladni Plate Resonance · Spectrograph · Atomic Viral Matrix · Iris Kaleidoscope · Firework Multi-Burst Grid · **Hybrid Mix** (layers several at once)

Every scene reacts to your music in its own way — bass drives the big movements, mids steer the motion, highs bring sparkle and detail, and beats trigger events like bursts, missiles and mutations.

## Make it yours

- **On your phone**, a simple quick bar does the driving: **MIC · FX · ◀ ▶ scene · RND · ⛶**. Pinch the screen to zoom the camera, drag two fingers sideways to rotate. The full control panel slides up as a sheet when you want to go deeper.
- **On desktop/tablet**, open the HUD for the full cockpit: scene mixing, beat automation, colour and gradient controls, custom palettes, and a **Text Projection** module — put your name, a message or your event's title right inside the visuals.
- **Beat FX** re-randomises the look on the beat; **2-Bar Colour FX** shifts the palette every two bars, so the visuals evolve with the track on their own.
- The square dots in the header switch the **Alpha Omega Collective channel** — Lime · Cobalt · Orange · Magenta · Teal — recolouring the interface (your visuals' colours stay yours).

**Desktop bonus — Sound Card mode:** instead of the mic, capture a browser tab's audio directly (Spotify/YouTube tab): click **Sound Card**, pick the tab, tick *Share audio*. Clean signal, zero room noise.

**Keyboard (desktop):** `H` tools · `M` mic · `S` sound card · `B` Beat FX · `R` randomise · `F` fullscreen · `1–9` scenes · `-` hybrid
